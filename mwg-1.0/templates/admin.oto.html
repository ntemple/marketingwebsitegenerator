Manage OTO Price/Buttons
This section is used to manage price settings for your oto products. Products are really "PAYMENT BUTTON" data needed to add to your oto products. These payment buttons are used by PayPal on your site for people to pay you. After you are done here, you can go to "Commission Structure" on the admin menu to set up the affiliate program when payments are made.

Name: is the name of the Product. You can change it to any custom name you like or leave it as is. Keep it similar to the membership name it relates to so that you do not get confused.

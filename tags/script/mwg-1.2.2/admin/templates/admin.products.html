Manage Products Price/Buttons
This section is used to manage price settings for your memberships. Products are really "PAYMENT BUTTON" data needed to add to your memberships. These payment buttons are used by PayPal on your site for people to pay you. After you are done here, you can go to "Commission Tiers" on the admin menu to set up the affiliate program when payments are made.

Name: is the name of the Product. You can change it to any custom name you like or leave it as is. Keep it similar to the membership name it relates to so that you do not get confused.

Unique ID for PayPal: is what people see on their receipt when they pay you or login to PayPal to see what they paid. Try to be descriptive, so even if people see this 1 year later they know what they purchased. i.e. : One year Golf Digest Elite Membership with Hand Held Software.

Clickbank Product Delivery ID: is the product ID in your Clickbank account. You have to set the return URL in clickbank: {site_full_url}clickbank.pay.return.php

2Checkout Product ID: is the product ID in your 2Checkout account. You have to set the return URL in clickbank: {site_full_url}2co.pay.return.php

Add a Ban

This is used to add a ban to restrict members access to your site. It can either be a total restriction meaning the visitor cannot view your site at all or it can be a signup restriction where he cannot signup to your site. For total restriction you can only add a ip ban, for signup you can add a restriction for every field that is required or present at signup (additionally to these you can also add a restriction to IP).

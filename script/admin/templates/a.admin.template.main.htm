What you would like to do?
View Currently Earning Transactions.

These are commissions your affiliates are currently earning for their most recent sales.
Change Status of commission.

Here is where change the status of your commissions from Pending to: To Be Paid, Canceled (Fraud or error), or change back current for to be paid. Or even delete (Delete Not Recommended unless it was for testing.)
To Be Paid - View amounts to pay to members and Get Mass Pay File

These are closed out commissions from a prior pay period that ended that your marked as current. You should for these commissions usually by the 6th - 15th of this current month once they are marked to be paid. You should mark all items to be paid on or around the 1st of every month. Make sure to "Mark As Paid" after you pay.
View past transactions

These are commissions you have already paid to your affiliates PayPal account. You have already paid these figures from past sales made by your members.
View cancelled transactions

These commissions were not paid for reasons of cancellations or fraud or error that you marked as canceled.

How do I do Mass Pay?

Choose "View amounts to pay to members" to get your PayPal Mass Pay File. make sure to Mark As Paid after you pay.

For PayPal

  • Step #1 - Download file

  • Step #2 - Save to Desktop

  • Step #3 - Login to PayPal

  • Step #4 - Click on Mass Pay Link at Button Navigation Links of PayPal

  • Step #5 - Click on "Make A Mass Payment Now!"

  • Step #6 - Upload Saved File On your Desktop

  • Step #7 - Make the rest of the payment as a normal PayPal payment
